HISTORY

Founded in 1995 with a passion for adventure, White Water Rafting has been delivering unforgettable experiences for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Our company began with just two rafts and a dream to share the beauty of river adventures with the world.

Today, we operate in 12 different locations with over 50 professional guides. We're proud to have served more than 100,000 customers who have trusted us for their adventure needs.
